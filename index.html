About Me

My love and passion for Nature has been an inspiration in my paintings. I love painting variety of subjects, especially Australian birds, seascapes and landscapes. Captivated by light and shadow, with the sun shining on a subject, I love to push my boundaries to try to recreate it in much of my work. I also enjoydoing some abstract work at times for varieties. Althogh my choosen medium is oil paints, I also enjoyusing other painting mediums 'En-Plein-Air' ( French term for painting outdoors) whenever possible.

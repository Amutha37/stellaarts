Welcome to my website

"I am an Australian artist, based in South West Sydney NSW.
I do hope you enjoy learning a bit about me and my artwork."

My love and passion for nature has been an inspiration in my paintings. I paint using oils on canvas. I love painting variety of subjects, especially Australian birds, seascapes, landscapes and portraits. I also enjoy doing some abstract work at times for variety. I enjoy going Plein-Air, (outdoor) painting whenever possible.

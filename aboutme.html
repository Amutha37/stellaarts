About Me


Born in Greece, I migrated here in Sydney from a young age. I am married with my husband of 31yrs together we have raised 3 sons.
My love for Nature is an inspiration in my work. I paint a variety of subjects especially Australian birds, landscapes and portraits in oils. I also use the pouring techniques for variety at times.

I worked in various firms in my past profession, mainly office work, which as a creative person leaves you feeling discontent and I was longing to fulfill my desire to create. So I would go to evening courses after work just to satisfy my creative needs. Includes were Floral art, Folk art, sewing, Jewellery etc.

After raising our sons being a stay at home mum, I worked in an Art shop which I believed help me find what I was created to be, an artist.

For me my happy place is my studio, being original and authentic is my goal. I constantly seek improvement in all my work to ensure my last painting is better then the previous. I enjoy staying connected to other artists, (going plein air painting where possible), also encouraging and motivating each other.

This is what drives me and my passion towards my art every day and why I'm so committed to it.

If you are interested in any of my work, or like me to commission a painting or custom portrait please contact with the links below.

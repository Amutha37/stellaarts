About Me


Hi, I'm Stella I am an Australian artist, born in Greece but migrated to Australia when I was young and I have been living in south west Sydney.

I love painting various subjects, including Australian birds, landscapes, seascapes and potraits. I also enjoy creating unique pieces of abstract work from time to time. My love and enthusiasm for nature is an inspiration in all my art work. I mainly paint using oils, but I also use other mediums, such as ???? at times. I enjoy being a part of a group with other artists on a regular basis. During this time, I share ideas, getting inspiration and actively involve in "plein air painting" (Frech term for painting outdoors) where possible. Being self motivated, I constantly seek improvements in my work and continue challenge myself to ensure every work I complete is better then the previous one. I also update my artist skill by constantly studying different techniques and methods.
